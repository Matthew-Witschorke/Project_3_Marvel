Marvel Universe



The Marvel Universe is a highly popular creation, and we had the desire to compare our favorite heros and villians.
By using a Kaggle Data Set we were able to display exactly this via the use of Sunburst Chart, Radar Charts, and
Bar Charts. As a guide we have a couple of our favorite Heros/Villians above to get your feet wet, but when you are
you can pick your own fight! If you need inspiration the explore tab is for you. Explore and have fun!
